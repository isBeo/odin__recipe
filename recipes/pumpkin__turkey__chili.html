Pumpkin Turkey Chili

This turkey pumpkin chili is absolutely delicious. It's quick and easy, too!

Ingredients:

  1. 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  2. 1 cup chopped onion
  3. ½ cup chopped green bell pepper
  4. ½ cup chopped yellow bell pepper
  5. 1 clove garlic, minced
  6. 1 pound ground turkey
  7. 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  8. 2 cups pumpkin puree
  9. 1 ½ tablespoons chili powder or more to taste
  10. ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
